状语从句 ​

  • 状语从句的本质来讲就是用不同的连词将几个分句连接起来，以表达分句之间特定的逻辑关系。
  • 状语从句的省略条件应同时满足以下两点，此时，可将从句主语和be动词省去
    • 当主句的主语和从句主语一致，或从句主语是it
    • 从句谓语中含有be动词
      • Metals expand when heated and contract when cooled.
      • The kind of tree grows favorably where wet. ≈ where it is wet
      • Although not a economist himself(≈Although he is not a economist himself), Dr. Smith has long been a severe critic of the government's economic policies.

时间状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

when ​

  • when充当时间连词时的意思主要包含两个方面

    • at the time(在...时刻)，从句谓语动词通常是短暂动词
    • during the time，从句谓语动词通常是延续动词

  • 常见主从时态搭配

    • 过去的动作

      • 表示从句动作先发生：一般过去时(主)+一般过去时/过去完成时(从)，

        • He left when I got there.
        • He left when I had got there.
        • 我到了以后他才离开

      • 表示主句动作先发生：过去完成时(主)+一般过去时(从)，

        • I had started my dinner when he left.

      • 一般过去时与过去进行时的互相搭配

        • 这种时态搭配下与主句从句没有关系，只与动作的长短的有关系，短暂动作一般用过去时，延续动作用过去进行时

          • 一般过去时(主)+过去进行时(从)

            • The door rang when(during the time) I was telephoning.

          • 过去进行时(主)+一般过去时(从)

            • I was telephoning when(at the time) suddenly the door rang. 此时when强调某个动作突然发生，所以在when引导的从句中可以添加suddenly一词

    • 将来的动作

      • 表示从句动作先发生

        • 一般将来时(主)+一般现在时(从)
          • I'll tell him about it when he comes back.
        • 一般将来时(主)+现在完成时(从)
          • 从句中使用现在完成时是为了强调：在从句动作结束之后，主句动作才开始发生，此时的从句动作一般都是需要持续一段时间的
            • I'll go when I have had my dinner. 我吃完饭就去

      • 表示主句动作先发生

        • 将来完成时(主)+一般将来时(从)
          • The performer will already have played the music when we get there. 一会我们到的时候，那位演奏家一定已经弹完那首曲子了

      • 将来进行时(主)+从句一般现在时， 类似上述的一般过去时与过去进行时的互相搭配

        • The performer will be playing the music when we get there. 一会我们到的时候，那位演奏家一定正在弹那首曲子

    • 现在一般的动作，主从句都用一般现在时，常用来阐明一个道理，名言警句中

      • When your attitudes are right, there is no barrier too high, no valley too deep, no dream too extreme, no challenge too great for me.
      • 常用的固定结构：when it comes to ... 当说到...
        • Doing your homework is a sure way to improve your test scores, and this is especially true when it comes to classroom tests.

  • whenever引导时间状语从句，表示“无论何时”，相当于when的强调形式

    • Whenever you say "I'm sorry", look the person right in the eyes`.

while ​

  • while充当时间连词是意思相当于during that time(在...期间)，与when的区别主要在于while通常只接延续动词
  • while除了表示时间外，还有另外三种意思：表示条件的“只要”，表示对比的“然而”，表示让步的“尽管”

as ​

  • as引导时间状语从句时，相当于while， 后面要接延续动词，主句延续动词，短暂动词皆可，一般翻译成**“正当...”，“随着...”，“一边...一边...”**

before ​

  • 引导时间状语从句类似when存在主从时态搭配，常见的是过去完成时(主)+一般过去时(从)
    • He had never gambled before the casino sent him a coupon for $20 worth of gambling. // 在...之前
  • 汉译特点
    • which took root in Europe long before people realized how diverse languages could be. // 英文中，before引导的从句一般接肯定形式，但在翻译成汉语时，往往处理成否定的意思 - 这一观点早在人们尚未认识到语言的千差万别以前就已在欧洲扎下了根
    • “Celebrate!” commanded the ads for the arthritis drug Celebrex, before we found out it increase the risk of heart attacks. // 前后对比关系，带有转折意味 - 治疗关节炎的药物Celebrex这个广告鼓动人们去“欢乐”，结果我们发现这种药品会加大罹患心脏病的危险‘
    • It took a few months of investigation before it became clear. // before引导的从句之前若接有一段时间，此时该从句可以翻译成“在多长时间之后才...” - 经过了几个月的调查之后，情况才开始变的明朗

after ​

  • 也存在各种主从时态搭配关系，常见的一种是一般过去时(主)+过去完成时(从)
    • I moved to America after I had learned English for ten years.

since ​

  • 详见完成时态关于since的阐述
    • 接短暂动词
    • 接延续动词
      • 一般过去时 - 从句动作“结束”以来，主句活动还在持续
      • 现在完成时 - 从句动作“开始”以来，主句活动还在持续
    • 动词延续性或短暂性不限，从句可用完成进行时态have been doing

until ​

  • 本质思维：一个(主句)动作一直持续到某一个时间点，具体说就是：until前面的主句或句子的谓语必须是延续性的，而until后面接的从句的谓语必须是短暂性动作或时间点
  • 句型结构
    • 肯定形式
      • until作为连词 - 主句的延续动词+until+包含短暂动词的从句
        • Wait until he comes back.
      • until作为介词 - 主句的延续动词+until+时间点(名词短语)
        • I will be away from my office until next Tuesday.
    • 否定形式 - 因为否定之后表示的是一个状态，而状态是延续的，也就是我们常说的not...until(直到...才)
      • until作为连词 - not意义+主句的短暂动词+until+包含短暂动词的从句
        • He didn't leave the office until his boss came back.
      • until作为介词 - not意义+主句的短暂动词+until+时间点(名词短语)
        • I didn't go to bed until 12 o'clock last night.
      • not...until常见变体
        • 变体一：until置于句首，只需将until后面的所有成分提到句首
          • Until he saw his mother, the little boy didn't smile.
        • 变体二：倒装结构
          • 在变体一基础上将否定词not提到句首
          • Not until he saw his mother, did the little boy smile.
        • 变体三：强调结构
          • 由结构三与强调结构It was ... that复合而成
          • It was not until he saw his mother that the little boy smiled.

"一......就"结构 ​

  • 从句动作先于主句动作
    • 常见引导词：as soon as, once, the minute, the moment, the instant, immediately, directly, instantly eg.
      • It began to rain as soon as(可替换成上面的连词) we began having dinner
    • 以上连词用法和句式结构一样，主从句时态一般一致
      • 表示将来的动作，主句一般将来时，从句一般现在时
      • 表示过去的动作，主从都一般过去时
  • 主句动作先于从句动作
    • 常见引导词：hardly...when，scarcely...when, no sooner...than
    • 以上连词用法和句式结构不一样，主从句时态一般也不一致
      • 一般多用来表示过去动作，主句过去完成时且常倒装，从句一般过去时，可与as soon as等主从互换
        • No sooner(Hardly/Scarcely) had I arrived home than(when) it began to rain.
        • It began to rain as soon as I arrived home.

by the time ​

  • 介词短词但起着连词的作用，后面可接一个从句，“在...之前”，强调“一个动作在另外一个动作发生之前已经完成”，主句要用完成时态
    • We had already left by the time the they arrived. // 表过去动作，
    • We will have already left by the time the they arrive. // 表将来动作

表时间的名词短语引导的时间状语从句 ​

  • 类似next time, every time, each time, the day, the week等名词短语也可当做连词来用，引导时间状语从句
    • Every time I listen to your advice, I get into trouble.
    • the first time vs for the first time

地点状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • 通常由where引导，“where+陈述句”从句可以放在句首或句末
    • Stay where you are.
    • 句首往往在翻译失去地点的含义了，要灵活处理
      • Where there is a will, there is a way. 有志者事竟成
  • wherever, anywhere, everywhere也可引导地点状语从句
    • Everywhere they appeared, there were ovations.(热烈欢迎)

原因状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • because

    • 语气最强，只有它才能回答why的问句，才能被only, just, perhaps等来修饰
    • 从句显得比主句重要，强调原因

  • since

    • 常翻译成“既然”，通常放在句首，表示已知的事实，不需要强调
    • 显得从句次要，主句重要，说明要做什么事

  • as

    • 与since用法差不多
    • 主从句位置平等

  • for

    • 表示推断的理由，是对前面分句内容加以解释或说明
    • 主从句位置平等

  • 表示因果关系的介词

    • because of, due to, owing to，后面接名词短语，不接从句

  • 其他复合连词表示原因

    • in that - "原因就在于"，引导的从句放在主句后面，通常是在作比较

      • Mercury differs from other industrial metals in that it is a liquid.

    • seeing (that), now (that), considering (that), given (that) - "鉴于某个事实，考虑到"

      • Now that you are sixteen, you can get a driver's license.

    • not that... but that... - 不是因为...而是因为...

      • I haven't finished writing the report yet, not that I'm lazy, but that I have no time.

    • inasmuch as, insomuch as, in as much as, in so much as - 可表原因，通常只用于书面语或演讲中

      • Inasmuch as the two government leaders could not reach an agreement, the possibilities for peace are still remote. // 由于双方政府的领导人无法达成一致，实现和平的可能性依然渺茫

  • because的否定转移，在主句是否定的情况下，because引导的从句的翻译可分为以下三种情况：

    • I don't study English just because it is so difficult. //只能按正常情况翻译

      • 我不学英文，因为它太难了

    • I don't study English just because I like to. // 只能按否定转移来翻译

      • 我学英文并不是因为我喜欢英文(否定了because引导的从句)
      • 如果正常来翻就是：我不学英文，就是因为我喜欢英文。明显不符合逻辑

    • 两种翻译都可，具体要看上下文

      • You don't love a woman because she is beautiful.
      • 你不爱一个女人，因为她太漂亮了
      • 你不是因为一个女人的美貌而爱他

    • 其实，否定转移并不局限与because引导从句，若是在口语中，一般句中重读什么词就否定什么词

      • I didn't come here to scold you. // 我来这里不是为了要批评你
      • He won't go to just any school. // 他可不愿意随随便便上一个学校就算了，重读any,won't 是否定any的，not any 不是任何一个，随便一个就可以的

    • 否定转移也为我们理解句子意思提供了一个方向，如果按照正常情况理解不通，就不妨考虑从否定转移的角度来翻译

目的状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • **so that, in order that, that，**另外在目的状语从句中常常含有may/might, can/could等情态动词

  • 若想强调目的状语从句，可将从句置于句首

    • In order that(So that) my roommate could study in peace and quiet, I turned off the TV.

  • 目的状语从句简化

    • 可用不定式结构来简化，上述三种对应的是：in order to, so as to do和to do

      • We climbed high in order to/so as to get a better view. // 注意inorder to放在句首句末都可，so as to不能置于句首
      • To acquire knowledge, one must study.

    • 具体指出不定式逻辑主语

      • I spoke slowly and clearly in order for the audience to understand me.

  • 其他表示目的的连词

    • Take an umbrella with you lest(以免，以防止)/for fear that(生怕)/in case(万一) it (should) rain. // should常常被省去

      • We had a meeting and talked the matter over face to face, for fear that there should be any misunderstanding. // for fear that或lest在某些句子中具有否定意义，≈in order that...not，即in order that there should not be any misunderstanding

    • The teacher raised his voice on purpose that(/in the hope that/for the purpose that/to the end that) the students in the back could hear more clearly.

结果状语从句 ​

  • 注意，结果状语从句都要放在主语之后

常见引导词 ​

  • so+adj./adv./分词+that，多数结果状语从句由so ... that引导

    • The newly described languages were often so strikingly different from the well studied languages that ...// adv.
    • We are so accustomed to adopting a mask before others that we end by being unable to recognize ourselves. // 分词
    • 偶尔也用so that来引导结果状语从句，注意与引导目的状语从句的so that区分，两者区别在于：
      • 逗号：引导结果状语时，往往会有逗号隔开主句和从句
      • 情态动词：结果状语中往往没有情态动词
        • ..., the continent may break entirely along some of these fissures**, so that** the hot spot initiates the formation of a new ocean.

  • such+名词+that

    • Diplomats and crabs are creatures who move in such a way that it is impossible to tell whether they are coming or going. // 外交官和螃蟹是行为方式相同的动物，......

    • such that引导结果状语从句时，such≈so+adj.,那这个adj.具体是什么要根据上下文判断，不过有时很难判断such可能含有的形容词意义，就是表示一种“就是如此”的感受

      • His anger was such that(≈so explosive that) he lost control of himself.

  • so vs. such

    • such后可以直接接名词

      • Such a force would give France a three-to-two advantage over Britain. // such+名词
      • Why should a state of such deep immobilization ever have evolved? // such+adj.+名词(单复数，不可数皆可)
      • The ministers used "splendid", "magnificent" and other such expressions to describe the King's new clothes. // 限定词(no,other,some,any,one,two,three等)+such+名词，five hundred such planes:五百架这样的飞机

    • so后面不可以直接接名词

      • The house is so expensive that I can't afford to buy it. // so+adj./adv.
      • This is so expensive a house that I can't afford to buy it. // so+adj.+a(n)+单数可数名词
      • The neighbors were making so much noise last night that I didn't sleep well. // so可以与表示数量的形容词连用如many,much,few,little，然后再接名词

  • 结果状语从句简化

    • 可以用不定式来简化，即so...as to, such... as to, such as to,注意不要漏掉as
      • He was such a fool as to believe him.
      • He was so foolish as to believe him.
        • 他是如此愚蠢竟然相信他
      • Her voice was such as to make everyone stare.

  • 为了强调，可以将主句中的so或者such引导的部分置于句首构成倒装结构

    • So terrible was the storm that the roofs were all ripped off.

  • Such a fool was he that he believed her.

    • Such is human nature that a great many people are often willing to sacrifice higher pay for the privilege of becoming white-collars workers. // 人性就是如此，......
    • Were the inventions so numerous devised to lessen the drudgery of washing clothes that the origin of the washing machine is unclear.

  • 其他引导结果状语从句的短语连接词

    • to the degree(that), to the extent(that), to such a degree(that), to such an extent(that)
      • The bereaved mother was grieved to the degree that she couldn't eat anything for 3 days.
      • To such an extent did he go on with his empty speech that some of us began to doze. // 倒装，他一直不断进行着自己空洞乏味的演讲，以至于我们当中都有人开始打瞌睡了

条件状语从句 ​

非真实条件语句 ​

  • 详见虚拟语气章节笔记

真实条件句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • 最常见的if(如果)/unless)(如果不，除非)

    • If winter comes, can spring be far behind?
    • only if 只有，if only 只要
    • must/have to do...if...be to do 如果...必须...，那么就不得不
      • The first two must be equal for all who are being compared, if any comparison in terms of intelligence is to be made.
      • If it doesn't rain within the next few weeks, the crops will have to be watered if they are to survive.

  • 其他表示条件的连词

    • suppose (that), supposing (that), providing/provided (that), so long as, as long as,on condition that, eg.
      • Knowledge helps you to reach the destination provided you know what the destination is.
      • A man is not old as long as he is seeking something.

  • 其他表示条件意味的句型

    • 祈使句/省略句+and=if 从句+主句

      • Spare the rod and spoil the child.=if you spare the rod, you'll spoil the child. // 孩子不打不成器

    • 祈使句/省略句+or/or else=unless 从句+主句

      • Give it back to me or (else) I'll tell your parents.=Unless you give it back to me, I'll tell your parents.

让步状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • 连词接完整的从句/形容词(≈省略句)/分词(≈省略句) - though, although, even though, even if, 主句前不可用but，但可用yet/still

    • Although he tried hard, (but ×，yet/still √)he failed.

  • 介词接名词(短语)/what引导的名词从句/动名词 - **despite, in spite of, for all, notwithstanding, in the event of **

    • In spite of his inexperience, he did a very good job.

  • 置于句首的while一般表示“尽管”，引导让步状语从句

  • no matter wh-(how) & -ever 形式引导让步状语从句，表示"无论",除了however，一般单独使用，而however后通常接adj./adv.

    • We decided to do so, whatever/no matter what happens.

    • Whoever may trouble you, I will help you.

    • But however amazed our descendants may be at how far from Utopia we wre, they will look just like us.

      • 从句谓语往往会有may或might。加强让步语气，相当于汉语“可能会多么的”, 有时会把may或might省去

    • Try as he will, no man breaks wholly loose from his first love, no matter who she (might) be... //

  • as引导倒装句表示让步 - adj./adv./分词/名词(无冠词)/短语+as+主语+谓语动词

    • Young as he is, he is knowledgeable.
    • Again as he failed in doing this experiment, he didn't lose his heart.
    • Praised as he was, he remained modest.
    • Child as he is, he is knowledgeable.
    • Lazy a boy as he is, he is kind to help others.
      • 注意粗体语序
      • 这里 as 可替换成that/though，但比较少用，不可以替换成although
    • 还可把动词原形置于句首：v. + as + 主语 + 助动词(may, might, will, would, can, could, do, does, did)
      • Fail as I did, I would never stop trying.
      • Lose money as I did, I got a lot of experience. // 若动词是及物动词，其宾语也要随着动词提前 - 尽管我费了不少钱，但是我获得了很多经验

  • as/so + adj. + as + 主谓结构，放在句首表让步

    • As much as John hates to do it, he must stay at home and study tonight.
    • As amusing and perceptive as graffiti may sometimes be, it has by no means won universal approval.

  • 句首用be的倒装句表让步

    • 表达两种或以上的情况，可改写成由whether引导的让步状语从句

      • All substances, be they gaseous, liquid or solid, are made up of atoms. // be they gaseous, liquid or solid=whether they may be gaseous, liquid or solid 不论...还是

    • 只表达一种情况，可改写成由however引导的让步状语从句

      • Be it ever so humble, there is no place like home. // Be it ever so humble,...=However humble it may be,... 不论多么

  • nevertheless - 不过，仍然，尽管如此;moreover - 而且，再者，此外; meanwhile - 在此期间，(于此)同时;

比较状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

as ... as ​

基本用法 ​
  • as+adj./adv.+as
  • as+adj.+a(n)+可数名词单数+as
    • 前一个as作为副词修饰形容词，形容词修饰名词，注意这里形容词置于不定冠词之前的特点
    • Rarely has a technological development had as great an impact on so many aspects of social, economic, and cultural development as the growth of electronics.
      • 正常语序 A technological development has rarely had as great an impact on ... as the growth of electronics.
      • 很少有哪项科技的发展能像电子技术的发展这样，对我们的社会，经济以及文化等诸多方面产生如此重要的影响
  • 在否定句中第一个as可用so代替
    • This room is not so large as the one we saw yesterday.
  • 保持句子平衡，从句倒装
    • as...as+助动词+主语
    • Small as it is, the ant is as much a creature as are all other animals on the earth.
      • as much ... as 在同等程度上，翻译成 和...一样
相关句型 ​
  • as much as结构，不能简单理解成“和...一样多”，它往往是表示两个事物之间程度的比较，表示“在同等程度上”，翻译时要灵活处理
    • The atmosphere is as much a part of the earth as are its soils and the waterof its lakes, rivers and oceans. // 地球周围的大气层，就如同地球上的泥土及湖泊、河流、海洋中的水一样，都是地球的组成部分。
  • not so much ... as结构用法特点
    • 常见结构：not A so much as B, not so much A as B
    • 基本含义：与其说A倒不如说B；是B，而不是A
    • A和B是两个被比较的平行结构，如：同为介词短语、动词不定式、名词短语或其他平行结构
      • The great use of a school education is not so much to teach you things as to teach you the art of learning.
      • The trumpet player was certainly loud, but I was not bothered by his loudness so much as by his lack of talent.
  • (just) as ..., so结构，表示类比，基本意思：“正如......”, 第二句可用倒装结构
    • Just as virtue is its own reward, so is vice its own punishment. //善有善报，恶有恶报
    • As you sow, so you will reap/so will you reap. // just可省略，种瓜得瓜，种豆得豆

than ​

基本用法 ​

  • 可比性 - 两个同类事物才能比较

    • In China the South generally receives much less snow than does the North. // 注意这里比较的对象是the South和the North， does the North = the North receives

  • 在有than的比较句子中，主句必须有比较级形式出现

  • 保持句子平衡，从句倒装

    • than+助动词+主语
    • In addition, far more Japanese workers expressed dissatisfaction with their jobs than did their counterparts(职务相当的人) in the 10 other countries surveyed.
      • 另外，据调查，不满意自己工作的日本员工比另外10个国家的员工人数要多得多
相关句型 ​
  • no more ... than结构
    • 基本意思：“同...一样不”，翻译时要表达出类比关系：先译主句 - “...不能...,就如同(than引导的从句)...不能”； 先译从句 - “正如(than从句)...不能...，所以...同样不能...”
    • 有个结构变体：not...any more than
    • 注意than后的句子为肯定形式，但要翻译成否定含义
      • The heart is no more intelligent than the stomach, for they are both controlled by the brain.
      • But his primary task is not to think about the moral code, which governs his activity, any more than a businessman is expected to dedicate his energies to an exploration of rule of conduct in business.

倍数比较 ​

  • 常见三种句型(以三倍three times为例)
    • "倍数+比较级" - A is three times bigger(或其他形容词比较级) than B.
    • "倍数+as...as" - A is three times as big(或其他形容词原级) as B.
    • "倍数+名词" - A is three times the size(或其他形容词对应的名词形式) of B.

the more..., the more ​

  • 用法要点
    • “the+比较级(从句)，the+比较级(主句)”结构，类似其他从句的“主句用将来时，从句用一般现在时”
    • 比较级部分都必须要在各自引导的分句中充当一定成分
    • 翻译时先译从句，再译主句
  • 比较翻译：你用词越准确，人们就越容易理解你的意思
    • The more exact you use your words, the easier people will understand you. ×
    • The more exactly you use your words, the more easily people will understand you.√
    • The more exact words you use, the easier it is for people to understand you.√
    • The more exact your words are, the more easily people will understand you.√
  • 有个不太常用的变体结构, 意思是一致的
    • The older she is, the more interested he is in her.
    • He is more interested in her, the older she is.

A is to B what/as C is to D结构 ​

  • A与B之间的关系就类似于C和D之间的关系，变体结构：what/as C is to D，so is A to B或A is to B
    • The people is to the people's army what(as) water is to fish. // 军民鱼水情
    • As(What) water is to fish, so is the people to the people's army(people is to the people's army).

其他 ​

  • She is more of a musician than her brother. // 她比她哥哥更像一个音乐家

方式状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • as, like, as if, as though, the way等，表示动作的方式
    • When in Rome, do as the Romans do. //入乡随俗
    • Do it the way you were taught.
    • He looks as if/as though he is an actor.
    • Sing like nobody's listening.
  • 重点as引导的方式状语从句

    • ..., look at an organic being as a savage looks at a ship, as at something whoolly beyond his comprehension. // 修饰句子的谓语部分

    • Some Asian nations and regions reiterated their opposition to a war, as did many of their citizens who worry Bush has already given up on hopes for a peaceful resolution to the crisis. // 倒装结构

      • 一些亚洲国家和地区重申了他们反对战争的立场，他们的许多公民也持同样的立场，因为他们担心小布什已经放弃了和平解决这一危机的希望。

    • 用于否定结构的特殊用法

      • as前面句子是否定的，as所在的句子本身是肯定的，通常会翻译成“不像”

        • He is not arguing, as many do, that we can no longer think straight because we do not talk proper. // 他并没有像许多人那样，认为因为我们话说得不好，就不会正确地思维了

      • as前面句子是肯定的，as所在的句子本身是否定的，通常会翻译成“但，然而”

        • He could love the child as he did not love Charles.

状语从句 ​

  • 状语从句的本质来讲就是用不同的连词将几个分句连接起来，以表达分句之间特定的逻辑关系。
  • 状语从句的省略条件应同时满足以下两点，此时，可将从句主语和be动词省去
    • 当主句的主语和从句主语一致，或从句主语是it
    • 从句谓语中含有be动词
      • Metals expand when heated and contract when cooled.
      • The kind of tree grows favorably where wet. ≈ where it is wet
      • Although not a economist himself(≈Although he is not a economist himself), Dr. Smith has long been a severe critic of the government's economic policies.

时间状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

when ​

  • when充当时间连词时的意思主要包含两个方面

    • at the time(在...时刻)，从句谓语动词通常是短暂动词
    • during the time，从句谓语动词通常是延续动词

  • 常见主从时态搭配

    • 过去的动作

      • 表示从句动作先发生：一般过去时(主)+一般过去时/过去完成时(从)，

        • He left when I got there.
        • He left when I had got there.
        • 我到了以后他才离开

      • 表示主句动作先发生：过去完成时(主)+一般过去时(从)，

        • I had started my dinner when he left.

      • 一般过去时与过去进行时的互相搭配

        • 这种时态搭配下与主句从句没有关系，只与动作的长短的有关系，短暂动作一般用过去时，延续动作用过去进行时

          • 一般过去时(主)+过去进行时(从)

            • The door rang when(during the time) I was telephoning.

          • 过去进行时(主)+一般过去时(从)

            • I was telephoning when(at the time) suddenly the door rang. 此时when强调某个动作突然发生，所以在when引导的从句中可以添加suddenly一词

    • 将来的动作

      • 表示从句动作先发生

        • 一般将来时(主)+一般现在时(从)
          • I'll tell him about it when he comes back.
        • 一般将来时(主)+现在完成时(从)
          • 从句中使用现在完成时是为了强调：在从句动作结束之后，主句动作才开始发生，此时的从句动作一般都是需要持续一段时间的
            • I'll go when I have had my dinner. 我吃完饭就去

      • 表示主句动作先发生

        • 将来完成时(主)+一般将来时(从)
          • The performer will already have played the music when we get there. 一会我们到的时候，那位演奏家一定已经弹完那首曲子了

      • 将来进行时(主)+从句一般现在时， 类似上述的一般过去时与过去进行时的互相搭配

        • The performer will be playing the music when we get there. 一会我们到的时候，那位演奏家一定正在弹那首曲子

    • 现在一般的动作，主从句都用一般现在时，常用来阐明一个道理，名言警句中

      • When your attitudes are right, there is no barrier too high, no valley too deep, no dream too extreme, no challenge too great for me.
      • 常用的固定结构：when it comes to ... 当说到...
        • Doing your homework is a sure way to improve your test scores, and this is especially true when it comes to classroom tests.

  • whenever引导时间状语从句，表示“无论何时”，相当于when的强调形式

    • Whenever you say "I'm sorry", look the person right in the eyes`.

while ​

  • while充当时间连词是意思相当于during that time(在...期间)，与when的区别主要在于while通常只接延续动词
  • while除了表示时间外，还有另外三种意思：表示条件的“只要”，表示对比的“然而”，表示让步的“尽管”

as ​

  • as引导时间状语从句时，相当于while， 后面要接延续动词，主句延续动词，短暂动词皆可，一般翻译成**“正当...”，“随着...”，“一边...一边...”**

before ​

  • 引导时间状语从句类似when存在主从时态搭配，常见的是过去完成时(主)+一般过去时(从)
    • He had never gambled before the casino sent him a coupon for $20 worth of gambling. // 在...之前
  • 汉译特点
    • which took root in Europe long before people realized how diverse languages could be. // 英文中，before引导的从句一般接肯定形式，但在翻译成汉语时，往往处理成否定的意思 - 这一观点早在人们尚未认识到语言的千差万别以前就已在欧洲扎下了根
    • “Celebrate!” commanded the ads for the arthritis drug Celebrex, before we found out it increase the risk of heart attacks. // 前后对比关系，带有转折意味 - 治疗关节炎的药物Celebrex这个广告鼓动人们去“欢乐”，结果我们发现这种药品会加大罹患心脏病的危险‘
    • It took a few months of investigation before it became clear. // before引导的从句之前若接有一段时间，此时该从句可以翻译成“在多长时间之后才...” - 经过了几个月的调查之后，情况才开始变的明朗

after ​

  • 也存在各种主从时态搭配关系，常见的一种是一般过去时(主)+过去完成时(从)
    • I moved to America after I had learned English for ten years.

since ​

  • 详见完成时态关于since的阐述
    • 接短暂动词
    • 接延续动词
      • 一般过去时 - 从句动作“结束”以来，主句活动还在持续
      • 现在完成时 - 从句动作“开始”以来，主句活动还在持续
    • 动词延续性或短暂性不限，从句可用完成进行时态have been doing

until ​

  • 本质思维：一个(主句)动作一直持续到某一个时间点，具体说就是：until前面的主句或句子的谓语必须是延续性的，而until后面接的从句的谓语必须是短暂性动作或时间点
  • 句型结构
    • 肯定形式
      • until作为连词 - 主句的延续动词+until+包含短暂动词的从句
        • Wait until he comes back.
      • until作为介词 - 主句的延续动词+until+时间点(名词短语)
        • I will be away from my office until next Tuesday.
    • 否定形式 - 因为否定之后表示的是一个状态，而状态是延续的，也就是我们常说的not...until(直到...才)
      • until作为连词 - not意义+主句的短暂动词+until+包含短暂动词的从句
        • He didn't leave the office until his boss came back.
      • until作为介词 - not意义+主句的短暂动词+until+时间点(名词短语)
        • I didn't go to bed until 12 o'clock last night.
      • not...until常见变体
        • 变体一：until置于句首，只需将until后面的所有成分提到句首
          • Until he saw his mother, the little boy didn't smile.
        • 变体二：倒装结构
          • 在变体一基础上将否定词not提到句首
          • Not until he saw his mother, did the little boy smile.
        • 变体三：强调结构
          • 由结构三与强调结构It was ... that复合而成
          • It was not until he saw his mother that the little boy smiled.

"一......就"结构 ​

  • 从句动作先于主句动作
    • 常见引导词：as soon as, once, the minute, the moment, the instant, immediately, directly, instantly eg.
      • It began to rain as soon as(可替换成上面的连词) we began having dinner
    • 以上连词用法和句式结构一样，主从句时态一般一致
      • 表示将来的动作，主句一般将来时，从句一般现在时
      • 表示过去的动作，主从都一般过去时
  • 主句动作先于从句动作
    • 常见引导词：hardly...when，scarcely...when, no sooner...than
    • 以上连词用法和句式结构不一样，主从句时态一般也不一致
      • 一般多用来表示过去动作，主句过去完成时且常倒装，从句一般过去时，可与as soon as等主从互换
        • No sooner(Hardly/Scarcely) had I arrived home than(when) it began to rain.
        • It began to rain as soon as I arrived home.

by the time ​

  • 介词短词但起着连词的作用，后面可接一个从句，“在...之前”，强调“一个动作在另外一个动作发生之前已经完成”，主句要用完成时态
    • We had already left by the time the they arrived. // 表过去动作，
    • We will have already left by the time the they arrive. // 表将来动作

表时间的名词短语引导的时间状语从句 ​

  • 类似next time, every time, each time, the day, the week等名词短语也可当做连词来用，引导时间状语从句
    • Every time I listen to your advice, I get into trouble.
    • the first time vs for the first time

地点状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • 通常由where引导，“where+陈述句”从句可以放在句首或句末
    • Stay where you are.
    • 句首往往在翻译失去地点的含义了，要灵活处理
      • Where there is a will, there is a way. 有志者事竟成
  • wherever, anywhere, everywhere也可引导地点状语从句
    • Everywhere they appeared, there were ovations.(热烈欢迎)

原因状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • because

    • 语气最强，只有它才能回答why的问句，才能被only, just, perhaps等来修饰
    • 从句显得比主句重要，强调原因

  • since

    • 常翻译成“既然”，通常放在句首，表示已知的事实，不需要强调
    • 显得从句次要，主句重要，说明要做什么事

  • as

    • 与since用法差不多
    • 主从句位置平等

  • for

    • 表示推断的理由，是对前面分句内容加以解释或说明
    • 主从句位置平等

  • 表示因果关系的介词

    • because of, due to, owing to，后面接名词短语，不接从句

  • 其他复合连词表示原因

    • in that - "原因就在于"，引导的从句放在主句后面，通常是在作比较

      • Mercury differs from other industrial metals in that it is a liquid.

    • seeing (that), now (that), considering (that), given (that) - "鉴于某个事实，考虑到"

      • Now that you are sixteen, you can get a driver's license.

    • not that... but that... - 不是因为...而是因为...

      • I haven't finished writing the report yet, not that I'm lazy, but that I have no time.

    • inasmuch as, insomuch as, in as much as, in so much as - 可表原因，通常只用于书面语或演讲中

      • Inasmuch as the two government leaders could not reach an agreement, the possibilities for peace are still remote. // 由于双方政府的领导人无法达成一致，实现和平的可能性依然渺茫

  • because的否定转移，在主句是否定的情况下，because引导的从句的翻译可分为以下三种情况：

    • I don't study English just because it is so difficult. //只能按正常情况翻译

      • 我不学英文，因为它太难了

    • I don't study English just because I like to. // 只能按否定转移来翻译

      • 我学英文并不是因为我喜欢英文(否定了because引导的从句)
      • 如果正常来翻就是：我不学英文，就是因为我喜欢英文。明显不符合逻辑

    • 两种翻译都可，具体要看上下文

      • You don't love a woman because she is beautiful.
      • 你不爱一个女人，因为她太漂亮了
      • 你不是因为一个女人的美貌而爱他

    • 其实，否定转移并不局限与because引导从句，若是在口语中，一般句中重读什么词就否定什么词

      • I didn't come here to scold you. // 我来这里不是为了要批评你
      • He won't go to just any school. // 他可不愿意随随便便上一个学校就算了，重读any,won't 是否定any的，not any 不是任何一个，随便一个就可以的

    • 否定转移也为我们理解句子意思提供了一个方向，如果按照正常情况理解不通，就不妨考虑从否定转移的角度来翻译

目的状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • **so that, in order that, that，**另外在目的状语从句中常常含有may/might, can/could等情态动词

  • 若想强调目的状语从句，可将从句置于句首

    • In order that(So that) my roommate could study in peace and quiet, I turned off the TV.

  • 目的状语从句简化

    • 可用不定式结构来简化，上述三种对应的是：in order to, so as to do和to do

      • We climbed high in order to/so as to get a better view. // 注意inorder to放在句首句末都可，so as to不能置于句首
      • To acquire knowledge, one must study.

    • 具体指出不定式逻辑主语

      • I spoke slowly and clearly in order for the audience to understand me.

  • 其他表示目的的连词

    • Take an umbrella with you lest(以免，以防止)/for fear that(生怕)/in case(万一) it (should) rain. // should常常被省去

      • We had a meeting and talked the matter over face to face, for fear that there should be any misunderstanding. // for fear that或lest在某些句子中具有否定意义，≈in order that...not，即in order that there should not be any misunderstanding

    • The teacher raised his voice on purpose that(/in the hope that/for the purpose that/to the end that) the students in the back could hear more clearly.

结果状语从句 ​

  • 注意，结果状语从句都要放在主语之后

常见引导词 ​

  • so+adj./adv./分词+that，多数结果状语从句由so ... that引导

    • The newly described languages were often so strikingly different from the well studied languages that ...// adv.
    • We are so accustomed to adopting a mask before others that we end by being unable to recognize ourselves. // 分词
    • 偶尔也用so that来引导结果状语从句，注意与引导目的状语从句的so that区分，两者区别在于：
      • 逗号：引导结果状语时，往往会有逗号隔开主句和从句
      • 情态动词：结果状语中往往没有情态动词
        • ..., the continent may break entirely along some of these fissures**, so that** the hot spot initiates the formation of a new ocean.

  • such+名词+that

    • Diplomats and crabs are creatures who move in such a way that it is impossible to tell whether they are coming or going. // 外交官和螃蟹是行为方式相同的动物，......

    • such that引导结果状语从句时，such≈so+adj.,那这个adj.具体是什么要根据上下文判断，不过有时很难判断such可能含有的形容词意义，就是表示一种“就是如此”的感受

      • His anger was such that(≈so explosive that) he lost control of himself.

  • so vs. such

    • such后可以直接接名词

      • Such a force would give France a three-to-two advantage over Britain. // such+名词
      • Why should a state of such deep immobilization ever have evolved? // such+adj.+名词(单复数，不可数皆可)
      • The ministers used "splendid", "magnificent" and other such expressions to describe the King's new clothes. // 限定词(no,other,some,any,one,two,three等)+such+名词，five hundred such planes:五百架这样的飞机

    • so后面不可以直接接名词

      • The house is so expensive that I can't afford to buy it. // so+adj./adv.
      • This is so expensive a house that I can't afford to buy it. // so+adj.+a(n)+单数可数名词
      • The neighbors were making so much noise last night that I didn't sleep well. // so可以与表示数量的形容词连用如many,much,few,little，然后再接名词

  • 结果状语从句简化

    • 可以用不定式来简化，即so...as to, such... as to, such as to,注意不要漏掉as
      • He was such a fool as to believe him.
      • He was so foolish as to believe him.
        • 他是如此愚蠢竟然相信他
      • Her voice was such as to make everyone stare.

  • 为了强调，可以将主句中的so或者such引导的部分置于句首构成倒装结构

    • So terrible was the storm that the roofs were all ripped off.

  • Such a fool was he that he believed her.

    • Such is human nature that a great many people are often willing to sacrifice higher pay for the privilege of becoming white-collars workers. // 人性就是如此，......
    • Were the inventions so numerous devised to lessen the drudgery of washing clothes that the origin of the washing machine is unclear.

  • 其他引导结果状语从句的短语连接词

    • to the degree(that), to the extent(that), to such a degree(that), to such an extent(that)
      • The bereaved mother was grieved to the degree that she couldn't eat anything for 3 days.
      • To such an extent did he go on with his empty speech that some of us began to doze. // 倒装，他一直不断进行着自己空洞乏味的演讲，以至于我们当中都有人开始打瞌睡了

条件状语从句 ​

非真实条件语句 ​

  • 详见虚拟语气章节笔记

真实条件句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • 最常见的if(如果)/unless)(如果不，除非)

    • If winter comes, can spring be far behind?
    • only if 只有，if only 只要
    • must/have to do...if...be to do 如果...必须...，那么就不得不
      • The first two must be equal for all who are being compared, if any comparison in terms of intelligence is to be made.
      • If it doesn't rain within the next few weeks, the crops will have to be watered if they are to survive.

  • 其他表示条件的连词

    • suppose (that), supposing (that), providing/provided (that), so long as, as long as,on condition that, eg.
      • Knowledge helps you to reach the destination provided you know what the destination is.
      • A man is not old as long as he is seeking something.

  • 其他表示条件意味的句型

    • 祈使句/省略句+and=if 从句+主句

      • Spare the rod and spoil the child.=if you spare the rod, you'll spoil the child. // 孩子不打不成器

    • 祈使句/省略句+or/or else=unless 从句+主句

      • Give it back to me or (else) I'll tell your parents.=Unless you give it back to me, I'll tell your parents.

让步状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • 连词接完整的从句/形容词(≈省略句)/分词(≈省略句) - though, although, even though, even if, 主句前不可用but，但可用yet/still

    • Although he tried hard, (but ×，yet/still √)he failed.

  • 介词接名词(短语)/what引导的名词从句/动名词 - **despite, in spite of, for all, notwithstanding, in the event of **

    • In spite of his inexperience, he did a very good job.

  • 置于句首的while一般表示“尽管”，引导让步状语从句

  • no matter wh-(how) & -ever 形式引导让步状语从句，表示"无论",除了however，一般单独使用，而however后通常接adj./adv.

    • We decided to do so, whatever/no matter what happens.

    • Whoever may trouble you, I will help you.

    • But however amazed our descendants may be at how far from Utopia we wre, they will look just like us.

      • 从句谓语往往会有may或might。加强让步语气，相当于汉语“可能会多么的”, 有时会把may或might省去

    • Try as he will, no man breaks wholly loose from his first love, no matter who she (might) be... //

  • as引导倒装句表示让步 - adj./adv./分词/名词(无冠词)/短语+as+主语+谓语动词

    • Young as he is, he is knowledgeable.
    • Again as he failed in doing this experiment, he didn't lose his heart.
    • Praised as he was, he remained modest.
    • Child as he is, he is knowledgeable.
    • Lazy a boy as he is, he is kind to help others.
      • 注意粗体语序
      • 这里 as 可替换成that/though，但比较少用，不可以替换成although
    • 还可把动词原形置于句首：v. + as + 主语 + 助动词(may, might, will, would, can, could, do, does, did)
      • Fail as I did, I would never stop trying.
      • Lose money as I did, I got a lot of experience. // 若动词是及物动词，其宾语也要随着动词提前 - 尽管我费了不少钱，但是我获得了很多经验

  • as/so + adj. + as + 主谓结构，放在句首表让步

    • As much as John hates to do it, he must stay at home and study tonight.
    • As amusing and perceptive as graffiti may sometimes be, it has by no means won universal approval.

  • 句首用be的倒装句表让步

    • 表达两种或以上的情况，可改写成由whether引导的让步状语从句

      • All substances, be they gaseous, liquid or solid, are made up of atoms. // be they gaseous, liquid or solid=whether they may be gaseous, liquid or solid 不论...还是

    • 只表达一种情况，可改写成由however引导的让步状语从句

      • Be it ever so humble, there is no place like home. // Be it ever so humble,...=However humble it may be,... 不论多么

  • nevertheless - 不过，仍然，尽管如此;moreover - 而且，再者，此外; meanwhile - 在此期间，(于此)同时;

比较状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

as ... as ​

基本用法 ​
  • as+adj./adv.+as
  • as+adj.+a(n)+可数名词单数+as
    • 前一个as作为副词修饰形容词，形容词修饰名词，注意这里形容词置于不定冠词之前的特点
    • Rarely has a technological development had as great an impact on so many aspects of social, economic, and cultural development as the growth of electronics.
      • 正常语序 A technological development has rarely had as great an impact on ... as the growth of electronics.
      • 很少有哪项科技的发展能像电子技术的发展这样，对我们的社会，经济以及文化等诸多方面产生如此重要的影响
  • 在否定句中第一个as可用so代替
    • This room is not so large as the one we saw yesterday.
  • 保持句子平衡，从句倒装
    • as...as+助动词+主语
    • Small as it is, the ant is as much a creature as are all other animals on the earth.
      • as much ... as 在同等程度上，翻译成 和...一样
相关句型 ​
  • as much as结构，不能简单理解成“和...一样多”，它往往是表示两个事物之间程度的比较，表示“在同等程度上”，翻译时要灵活处理
    • The atmosphere is as much a part of the earth as are its soils and the waterof its lakes, rivers and oceans. // 地球周围的大气层，就如同地球上的泥土及湖泊、河流、海洋中的水一样，都是地球的组成部分。
  • not so much ... as结构用法特点
    • 常见结构：not A so much as B, not so much A as B
    • 基本含义：与其说A倒不如说B；是B，而不是A
    • A和B是两个被比较的平行结构，如：同为介词短语、动词不定式、名词短语或其他平行结构
      • The great use of a school education is not so much to teach you things as to teach you the art of learning.
      • The trumpet player was certainly loud, but I was not bothered by his loudness so much as by his lack of talent.
  • (just) as ..., so结构，表示类比，基本意思：“正如......”, 第二句可用倒装结构
    • Just as virtue is its own reward, so is vice its own punishment. //善有善报，恶有恶报
    • As you sow, so you will reap/so will you reap. // just可省略，种瓜得瓜，种豆得豆

than ​

基本用法 ​

  • 可比性 - 两个同类事物才能比较

    • In China the South generally receives much less snow than does the North. // 注意这里比较的对象是the South和the North， does the North = the North receives

  • 在有than的比较句子中，主句必须有比较级形式出现

  • 保持句子平衡，从句倒装

    • than+助动词+主语
    • In addition, far more Japanese workers expressed dissatisfaction with their jobs than did their counterparts(职务相当的人) in the 10 other countries surveyed.
      • 另外，据调查，不满意自己工作的日本员工比另外10个国家的员工人数要多得多
相关句型 ​
  • no more ... than结构
    • 基本意思：“同...一样不”，翻译时要表达出类比关系：先译主句 - “...不能...,就如同(than引导的从句)...不能”； 先译从句 - “正如(than从句)...不能...，所以...同样不能...”
    • 有个结构变体：not...any more than
    • 注意than后的句子为肯定形式，但要翻译成否定含义
      • The heart is no more intelligent than the stomach, for they are both controlled by the brain.
      • But his primary task is not to think about the moral code, which governs his activity, any more than a businessman is expected to dedicate his energies to an exploration of rule of conduct in business.

倍数比较 ​

  • 常见三种句型(以三倍three times为例)
    • "倍数+比较级" - A is three times bigger(或其他形容词比较级) than B.
    • "倍数+as...as" - A is three times as big(或其他形容词原级) as B.
    • "倍数+名词" - A is three times the size(或其他形容词对应的名词形式) of B.

the more..., the more ​

  • 用法要点
    • “the+比较级(从句)，the+比较级(主句)”结构，类似其他从句的“主句用将来时，从句用一般现在时”
    • 比较级部分都必须要在各自引导的分句中充当一定成分
    • 翻译时先译从句，再译主句
  • 比较翻译：你用词越准确，人们就越容易理解你的意思
    • The more exact you use your words, the easier people will understand you. ×
    • The more exactly you use your words, the more easily people will understand you.√
    • The more exact words you use, the easier it is for people to understand you.√
    • The more exact your words are, the more easily people will understand you.√
  • 有个不太常用的变体结构, 意思是一致的
    • The older she is, the more interested he is in her.
    • He is more interested in her, the older she is.

A is to B what/as C is to D结构 ​

  • A与B之间的关系就类似于C和D之间的关系，变体结构：what/as C is to D，so is A to B或A is to B
    • The people is to the people's army what(as) water is to fish. // 军民鱼水情
    • As(What) water is to fish, so is the people to the people's army(people is to the people's army).

其他 ​

  • She is more of a musician than her brother. // 她比她哥哥更像一个音乐家

方式状语从句 ​

常见引导词 ​

  • as, like, as if, as though, the way等，表示动作的方式
    • When in Rome, do as the Romans do. //入乡随俗
    • Do it the way you were taught.
    • He looks as if/as though he is an actor.
    • Sing like nobody's listening.
  • 重点as引导的方式状语从句

    • ..., look at an organic being as a savage looks at a ship, as at something whoolly beyond his comprehension. // 修饰句子的谓语部分

    • Some Asian nations and regions reiterated their opposition to a war, as did many of their citizens who worry Bush has already given up on hopes for a peaceful resolution to the crisis. // 倒装结构

      • 一些亚洲国家和地区重申了他们反对战争的立场，他们的许多公民也持同样的立场，因为他们担心小布什已经放弃了和平解决这一危机的希望。

    • 用于否定结构的特殊用法

      • as前面句子是否定的，as所在的句子本身是肯定的，通常会翻译成“不像”

        • He is not arguing, as many do, that we can no longer think straight because we do not talk proper. // 他并没有像许多人那样，认为因为我们话说得不好，就不会正确地思维了

      • as前面句子是肯定的，as所在的句子本身是否定的，通常会翻译成“但，然而”

        • He could love the child as he did not love Charles.
